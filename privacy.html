Gradix Technologies values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1 Information We Collect

We collect personal and technical information to serve you better:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company Name
  • Project Requirements

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Browser Information
  • Device Information
  • Website Analytics Data

2 How We Use Information

We process information for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Respond to your inquiries, schedule consultation calls, and outline project proposals.
  • Provide, engineer, deliver, and continuously optimize our software and digital services.
  • Communicate important project updates, deployment schedules, and architectural details.
  • Enhance website performance, analyze speed metrics, and enforce system security measures.
  • Improve general user experience, responsiveness, and page layout architectures.

3 Cookies

We may use cookies, local storage sessions, and technical analytics tools to improve website accessibility, recognize preferences across dark and light modes, and compile visitor behavior maps. You can manage or disable cookie configurations anytime through your specific browser options.

4 Information Sharing

We strictly **do not** sell, rent, lease, trade, or distribute your personal information to third-party marketing companies. Data is only shared in direct situations where it is required by law, necessary to protect system safety, or essential for integrations with trusted infrastructure partners to deliver your specific project assets.

5 Data Security

We implement premium physical, administrative, and technical security safeguards (including SSL encryption protocols, firewalls, and credential hashing) to protect your personal and project information. However, please note that no method of internet-based storage or data transmission is 100% impenetrable.

6 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain external links to other industry platforms, tech repositories, social media portals, or client showcases. We do not operate these external systems and are not responsible for their individual content accuracy or privacy practices.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your regional jurisdiction, you possess the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • **Access**: Request direct access to and clear details about the personal information we hold about you.
  • **Correction**: Request prompt updates or corrections to any inaccurate, outdated, or incomplete data.
  • **Erasure**: Request the complete deletion or removal of your personal data from our active storage files where applicable.

8 Children's Privacy

Our development platforms and services are not structured for, nor do we knowingly collect personal data from, children under the age of 13. If we discover that personal details have been uploaded from a child under 13, we will purge it immediately.

9 Policy Updates

We reserve the right to revise this Privacy Policy at our discretion. Changes and updates will be highlighted directly on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage visitors to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our data protection standards.
